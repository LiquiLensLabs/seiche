# Code of Conduct

Seiche is a small project about honest measurement. The community standard is
the same as the technical standard: be accurate, be direct, and do not
misrepresent things.

## Expected

- Criticize methods as hard as you like. Adversarial review is a gift here,
  and finding a real flaw in the record is the most valued contribution there
  is.
- Be respectful to people while doing it. Attack the number, not the person.
- Disclose conflicts of interest when commenting on methodology, positioning,
  or data sources.

## Not tolerated

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks of any kind, including on
  the basis of gender identity, caste, religion, nationality, or anything
  else about who a person is.
- Misrepresenting the project's outputs, or presenting doctored versions of
  the record as genuine.
- Sustained bad faith: sealioning, brigading, or wasting maintainer time.

## Enforcement

Reports go to desk@seiche.info and are read by the maintainer. Consequences
range from a warning to a permanent ban from project spaces, at the
maintainer's discretion. Reports are kept confidential.

Adapted in spirit from the Contributor Covenant, shortened to fit a one
maintainer project that values blunt technical criticism.
